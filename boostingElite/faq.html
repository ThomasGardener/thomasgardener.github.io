Frequently Asked Questions

Order Related

  • What platform do you boost on

    Currently on PC. We'll be looking to expand to consoles in the future if the demand is high enough.

  • What times do you normally boost from

    Boost times are always from 05:00-15:00GMT, with additional time put in afterwards depending on the day.

  • Do you boost in solo, duo or squads?

    Currently we're boosting solo for wins and other services unless specified otherwise. We may however do a few duo/squad games depending on the time of day (however if you'd prefer purely solo that can be easily arranged)

  • How will I know the progress of my order?

    You'll be able to contact us at all times about the status of your order and we will keep you informed

  • How are the wins service carried out?

    We will play the amount of games needed to get you the required wins, with each of our boosters having a win rate of +50% it can be done suprisingly quick

Account Related

  • How secure/private will my account be

    Given that we only ever give your information to one sole booster on our site, you will not have to worry about having anyone else with access to your details. We have completed numerous orders and have yet to hear or have anyone that has had their account banned

  • Can I play on the account while boosting?

    If you wish to play during the boost times 05:00-15:00GMT, we can easily set it up. It may add additional time to the service depending on the duration

Payment Related

  • Is paypal your only method of recieving payments?

    Currently we only accept paypal. However We'll be looking at other payment options in the near future.

  • How do you charge for your leveling service that costs 500+ Euros?

    We'll split the 1-65 and 1-85 levels into sections in such a way that you will pay the amount divided into 4 sections for example for our 1-65 service: 1-35, 35-50, 50-60, 60-65. In total equaling the same amount. This is purely to satisfy customers to ensure that you are getting the order you paid for
